The Benefits of Running

Many people dread running, but there is plenty of evidence that supports reasons to lace up your sneakers and hit the road. There are several benefits to going on a quick run even just a few times a week. Three of my favorite benefits are:
  • it makes you happier
  • strengthens your bones
  • keeps you fit

Accoding to Runners World Magazine, just 30 minutes of running can increase your mood instantly. Also, running decreases anxiety and depression symptoms.

Obviously running burns many calories, about a 100 a mile. The more calories you burn the more food you can eat later!
